Top Favourite Movies of Abhishek

My top 3 movies of all time

Munna Bhai MBBS

This is of the best creations of Rajkumar Hirani. It is a comedy movie. In this movie it show the protagonist Munna, who is a local gangster, who moved by the insult of his father by his friend who was a doctor, takes a pledge to become a doctor.

3 idiots

This is a Rajkumar Hirani movie which takes us to the life of students of engineering colleges and shows the enormous amount of stress they goes through. It shows 3 friend raju,farhan and rancho and their journey to this world of competence and how they find meaning to their life.

Taare Zameen Par

This is another great Rajkumar Hirani movie, it takes you throught daily busy life of nuclear family and the continous burden of expectations on thier childrens. This movie shows a boy ishaan who crumbled by the burden of his family's expectations becomes dyslexic and his journey to find meaning of his life through his passion.
